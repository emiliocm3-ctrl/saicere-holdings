About

Vision

What We Do

Portfolio Management

Venture Lab

Our Values

Purpose & Congruence

  • Purpose is our shared direction and decision-making criterion.
  • We evaluate whether our actions move us closer to or further from the common goal.
  • Congruence is coherence between our values, words, and actions.
  • Efficiency without purpose breeds apathy.
  • We don't believe in the deferred life plan — we build an aligned life starting today.

Collaboration & Connection

  • We collaborate not out of obligation, but from a shared vision.
  • Openness and leaving ego at the door are essential.
  • We value empathy, positive attitude, and cultural alignment.
  • Ego, closed-mindedness, and lack of emotional intelligence disconnect us.
  • Our contribution is direction, perspective, and reflection.

Curiosity & Innovation

  • Intellectual and creative curiosity drives us.
  • Learning matters more than being right.
  • Innovation means creating more value — improving or building something new.
  • We think in spectrums, not binary answers.
  • Our mental framework: Why → What if → How.

Ambition & Freedom

  • Ambition means moving forward and refusing to settle.
  • Freedom is the ability to decide how we use our time and energy.
  • Freedom has limits — it must not negatively affect others.
  • We pursue growth aligned with our shared vision.
